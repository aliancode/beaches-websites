Morocco's Most Beautiful Beaches

Explore the sun, surf, and serenity of Morocco's coastal gems

Agadir Beach

Agadir is Morocco’s most modern beach resort, perfect for relaxing and enjoying sunny weather all year round. With wide sandy shores and modern facilities, it's a favorite among both tourists and locals.

Taghazout

Taghazout is a charming surf town just north of Agadir. It’s internationally known for its world-class surfing waves, peaceful atmosphere, and bohemian vibe. Ideal for surfers and backpackers seeking authentic coastal life.

Legzira Beach

Legzira is famous for its massive natural rock arches carved by the ocean. Located between Mirleft and Sidi Ifni, it’s a quiet and breathtaking destination, especially at sunset. A paradise for photographers and nature lovers.
