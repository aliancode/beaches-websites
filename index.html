General Questions
What is the best time to visit Moroccan beaches?
The best time to visit Moroccan beaches is from April to October when temperatures are warm and rainfall is minimal. The peak season is July-August, while spring and autumn offer fewer crowds.
Do I need a visa to visit Morocco?
Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Many countries (including US, EU, UK, Canada) can enter Morocco visa-free for up to 90 days. Always check with your local Moroccan embassy before traveling.

Booking Questions

What is your cancellation policy?
We offer free cancellation up to 14 days before your scheduled arrival. Cancellations within 14 days may incur a fee depending on the property or activity booked.
Can I modify my booking after confirmation?
Yes, most bookings can be modified up to 48 hours before your scheduled date. Please contact our customer support team for assistance with modifications.

Location Questions

Which Moroccan beach is best for families?
Agadir and Saidia are excellent for families with calm waters and plenty of amenities. For older kids, Taghazout offers great surfing opportunities with beginner-friendly waves.
Are Moroccan beaches safe for swimming?
Most popular beaches are safe for swimming, but always check local conditions. Some Atlantic beaches can have strong currents. We recommend swimming at beaches with lifeguards during patrol hours.

Activity Questions

What water sports are available?
Popular activities include surfing (especially in Taghazout), jet skiing, parasailing, paddleboarding, and snorkeling. Most beach resorts offer equipment rentals and lessons.
Do I need to book activities in advance?
During peak season (June-August), we recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance. For private tours or specialized activities, book even earlier. Off-season, you can often book a day or two in advance.
